about_me.txt

Aspiring audio software developer studying at University of Toronto.

I have a huge passion for both coding and music production. Because of that, my goal is to combine both my passions and become an audio software developer. I am proficient in several programming languages such as Python, Java, C, C++, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I am also able to manipulate digital audio at a high level using DAWs, as well as at a low level using the C++ JUCE framework. Furthermore, I have experience working as a computer technician at Lanner Electronics Inc., where I took on various tasks such as computer assembly, quality control, and RMA depending on the company's current needs. My experiences made me become adaptable, detail-oriented, and efficient in a team environment.

Delay/Chorus Plugin



An audio plugin that simulates a delay or chorus effect. Users can switch between the two effects through a dropdown menu and adjust the parameters through the parameter knobs in the GUI. It also features a fully functional preset manager for users to save and load presets.
